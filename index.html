Now that students have taken the state math tests, we will return to computer science. Today we will begin working on final projects, where students will demonstrate and apply some aspect of computer technology. The first step is to choose a topic and develop a written proposal. Students may work in groups of 2 or 3 if desired. When the proposal is approved, students will collaborate using Microsoft Teams to produce a product such as a web page, a presentation on a technological topic, a game, a survey with a presentation of results, an app, a spreadsheet of market transactions, etc. The proposal should be delivered as a Microsoft Word document in students' shared OneDrive folder. The proposal should begin with a summary of the goals of the project. Next, the proposal should identify the technologies that will be used to develop the product. The proposal should identify how these technologies will be implemented. For students working in a group, responsibilities for specific tasks within the group should be identified. The proposal should discuss any difficulties that may impact the final product. Finally, the proposal should conclude with the expected date and format on which the project will be delivered or presented.
